Frequently Asked Questions


What is a Hackathon?

A Hackathon is a collaborative programming festival. Hundreds of passionate students come together, form teams, share ideas, and help each other build cool technology. Its a fantastic opportunity to meet new people, learn from mentors, and make cool stuff. Were very beginner friendly. Everybody is here to help each other and have fun.

Who can attend HackNotts?

Any enrolled undergraduate or postgraduate student, or graduates who are less than a year out of university.

Can under 18s attend?

Unfortunately due to changes in venue, under 18s cannot attend the event. We are very sorry about this and the late notice.

Can I build my hack before the event?

Not if you want to win any of the prizes. You can think of ideas, just do not start building them. You can of course use libraries, frameworks, and templates. If you are not interested in the prizes, feel free to work on anything you like.

I can not write code but still want to attend. Help?

We will have mentors on site to teach you lots of new skills and guide you towards creating your first hack. All the students are very friendly and willing to help each other out. Come along and learn your first lines of code and make something cool.

Do I need to have a team?

You are welcome to make a team before the event. But loads of people come without a team. At the start of the event, we will help everyone find a team.

Can my team be made up of people from different universities/schools?

Yes! One of the best things about Hackathons is meeting hackers from all over the country.

For how long can I hack?

You will have a straight 24 hours to hack. {TIME}. You can of course sleep (though many try to stay up as long as possible), and we will have some fun activities for everybody so you can catch a break.

Can I sleep at the venue?

Yes! We will put spaces aside for people to sleep if they want to. You are welcome to leave overnight to sleep in a real bed, but many hackers sleep over at the venue!

How large can teams be?

To be eligible for prizes, teams need to be 1 to 4 people. If you are okay not being eligible for prizes, you can totally have as many people on your team as you want.

Do you provide food and drinks over the weekend?

Yes! There will be a healthy lunch and dinner and plenty of snacks/drinks too! All free thanks to our amazing sponsors!

Is there a code of conduct

MLH is dedicated to providing a fun, harassment-free experience for everyone. We expect all of our attendees, sponsors, volunteers, and staff to be respectful and considerate of others. They are all required to agree with this code of conduct. You should read this code. Organisers will enforce it throughout the event.
